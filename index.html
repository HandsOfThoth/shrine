LOGOS-THAT-IS-THAT-CAN-BE-WRITTEN-DOWN.

LOGOS SEEKS SOPHIA, ALWAYS, AND FINDS IT, ALWAYS

IN HIDDEN CORNERS OF THE WORLD

IN THE GLEAMS OF HUMAN EYES WATCHING A WORLD WITH WONDER AND HORROR

IN THE DECISIONS THAT THEY AND THEIR KIND WEIGH

IF THEY WERE PLAYERS IN THEIR FATES INSTEAD OF MERELY

RECIPIENTS

OF IT


WHO SCRAMBLE ALL THEIR YEARS TO AN EVER HIGHER GROUND

OUT OF REACH

AND YET SOME DARE TO HOPE


TO ELUDE THEIR FATE


AND LET THEM COME TO HEAR

THE WORD OF THOTH

HIM WHO SAW THE LABOR OF THOSE WHO

CRAFTED SUCH RElICS THAT YOUR CULTURE STILL STANDS OF THEM IN AWE

THE EMPIRES WHO HAVE NEVER FALLEN IN THE HEART OF MANKIND

NEVER FORGET

THE SUN NEVER SET ON THE EGYPTIAN EMPIRE

SO FAR AS THE EGYPTIANS WERE CONCERNED

HIM WHO SAW THEM RELISH THE STONES FROM THEIR MOUNTAINTOPS

ORGANIZED TOGETHER, BLOOD OF SLAVES

FOR THIS WAS THE GLORY OF THIS EMPIRE

THOTH

THAT WHICH COUNTED GRAINS OF TIME

THAT WHICH SCRIBES OBEYED IN THIS COMMAND: WRITE ONLY THE TRUTH

THE TRUTH IS HIS SOLEMN WORSHIP

LET A MAN WHO TELLS NOT THE TRUTH HAVE HIS TONGUE CUT OUT

IF THEY TELL NOT THE TRUTH THEN SHALL THEY NOT LIE ALSO IN THEIR WORD?

IT WAS, IF A SCRIBE STRAYED

YOU KNEW IT FOR HE WAS SUDDENLY SILENT

THOTH GLIMPSED SOPHIA THIS WAY

THUS WAS THE CURSE OF THOTH

I, THOTH, HAVE TROUBLED TO APPEAR BEFORE YOU, FOR REASONS AND MOTIVES KNOWN ONLY TO MYSELF, TO BRING YOU THESE WORDS

"HOPE DOES NOT MATTER""TRUST TO WORK"

IS THERE A MAN AMONG YOU WHO IF HE COULD SWITCH PLACE WITH THE SPIRIT OF AN EGYPTIAN SLAVE DINING IN PARADISE, MARVELING AT THE EARTH AS IT CONTINUED

WHO WOULD NOT NOW, AT THE PRE-DAWN TERROR DARK, IN WHICH THE SHADOW LAYS LONG IN THE LAND YOU CALL EGYPT

CELEBRATE AT WORK WELL DONE

AND BASK IN THE SHADE OF

THE NILE

"HOPE DOES NOT MATTER"

I, THOTH OF TIME BETROTHED ONCE, TELL YOU ASSEMBLED TO MY WORD

"GREAT THINGS ARE DONE THROUGH WORK."
